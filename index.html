The Humans

Humans are known to others primarily by their homelands.
The three great human cultures are the Midlanders,
the Wastelanders and the Northlanders.

  • Midlander
  • Northlander
  • Wastelander
The Elves

The ancient elves have lived across the world of Sapience.
The Grey Elves are noble, wise and withdrawn.
The Dark Elves are hierarchical, cunning and always in pursuit of power.
The Wild Elves, stern and resolute, have a love and respect for nature.

  • Dark Elf
  • Grey Elf
  • Wild Elf
The Dwarves

The pious and formal Deep Beards are a contrast
to the impetuous and clan-focused High Beards.

  • Deep Beard
  • High Beard
The Dahn

Those Orcs and Goblins who eschew the savage ways
of the majority of their type are known as the Dahn.

  • Goblin
  • Orc
The Wyrd

Of all the mortal races of Sapience, there are none more shrouded in mystery than the Wyrd.
